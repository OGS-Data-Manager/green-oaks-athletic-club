The Club

Play, learn, and have a blast

Green Oaks Athletic Club is an after-school sports program at Oak Grove School, run by one of the school's own PE teachers — Coach Izenstark. Every week features a different sport, so kids get to try a little of everything.

The club runs in seasonal sessions through the school year. Each session offers a rotating lineup of sports and games, with separate groups for K–2 and 3rd–5th graders so every kid gets an age-right experience.

It's not a competitive league. It's about moving, learning the fundamentals, and having fun with friends after the school day.

Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

Does my child need sports experience?

Not at all. The club is built for all skill levels — whether your child is a budding athlete or just wants to run around after school, they'll fit right in.

What sports do the kids play?

A different one every Friday. This fall's K–2 lineup is kickball, soccer, "bennis" (baseball with a tennis racket), basketball, floor hockey, and capture the flag. See the full schedule.

How are the groups split?

K–2 and 3rd–5th graders play in separate groups so the games fit each age's size and skill level.

What if the weather is bad?

The club runs rain or shine. Depending on the day, a session may be held outdoors or in the Junior High Gym — and if the weather isn't suitable for outside, the gymnasiums are available, so club still meets. It's not cancelled for weather.

Will sessions be held indoors or outdoors?

It depends on the day and the activity. When the weather's nice, a session may be held outside; when it isn't suitable for outdoors, the school gymnasiums are available so club still meets. Either way, kids are active for the full 3:30–4:30 PM.

When is the K–2 fall session?

Six Fridays, August 28 through October 2, 2026, meeting 3:30–4:30 PM every week. Note: Friday, September 18 is an early-release day at school, but club still meets at the regular 3:30 PM — teachers aren't dismissed early, so the time doesn't change.

How much does it cost, and how do I pay?

$120 for the full six-week session — just $20 a week. You register and pay online by card in one step, right from this site, and you'll get an emailed receipt on the spot. Please note the fee is non-refundable, including for any weeks your child misses.

Is this session only for K–2?

Yes — Fall Session #1 is for grades K–2 only. A session for grades 3–5 will be announced separately — add your name to the interest list and you'll be the first to hear.

Who do I contact with questions?

coaches@greenoaksathleticclub.com
