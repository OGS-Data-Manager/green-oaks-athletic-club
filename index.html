About the Club

Green Oaks Athletic Club is a new after-school sports program at Oak Grove School, led by Coach Izenstark and Coach Callahan. Each week features a different sport — your child will play, learn, and have a blast. No experience needed, just a love of being active!

The club runs in 6-week sessions. Each session offers a rotating lineup of sports and activities, with separate groups for K–2 and 3rd–5th graders so every kid gets an age-appropriate experience.

Soccer, basketball, kickball, flag football, “bennis,” and more. Your child gets exposure to a variety of activities.

Age-Appropriate Groups

K–2 and 3rd–5th graders play separately so activities are tailored to each age group's abilities.

All Skill Levels Welcome

This isn't a competitive league. It's about getting active, learning fundamentals, and having fun.

What to Bring

Frequently Asked Questions

Does my child need sports experience?

Not at all! The club is designed for all skill levels. Whether your child is a budding athlete or just wants to run around after school, they'll fit right in.

Do I sign up for individual weeks or the whole session?

You register and pay for the full 6-week session. Your child participates in all six weeks.

What if the weather is bad?

Activities move indoors to the school gyms during rain or extreme heat. The club runs rain or shine.

How many kids are in each group?

Each grade band (K–2 and 3rd–5th) is capped at 25 kids to keep things fun and safe.

How does pickup work?

Kids go straight from class to the gym at 3:30 PM. Pickup is at 4:30 PM at the school. More details will be provided after registration.

Will there be more sessions after this one?

Session 1 runs through the end of this school year. Future sessions with new sports and activities are planned — stay tuned!
